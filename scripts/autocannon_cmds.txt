Linux/macOS autocannon examples:

# Simple baseline (50 concurrent clients, 30s duration)
npx autocannon -c 50 -d 30 -p 10 http://localhost:3001/api/analyze > scripts/reports/autocannon-basic.txt

# JSON output for CI
npx autocannon -c 50 -d 30 -p 10 -j http://localhost:3001/api/analyze > scripts/reports/autocannon-report.json

# Run with 100 clients to stress test (use with caution)
npx autocannon -c 100 -d 60 -p 10 http://localhost:3001/api/analyze > scripts/reports/autocannon-heavy.txt

# Autocannon with piped artillery-like report
npx autocannon -c 50 -d 30 -p 10 http://localhost:3001/api/analyze | tee scripts/reports/autocannon-console.txt

# Notes:
# - Run load tests in an isolated environment (staging) and not against production endpoints.
# - Adjust concurrency (-c) and duration (-d) according to your environment capacity.
